About Mathebula Hotel

Mathebula Hotel Pretoria West is situated in the trendy and upmarket suburb of Elandspoort and offers sophisticated accommodation in Pretoria.

The 119 contemporary rooms were designed to offer fashionable, yet comfortable accommodation and rooms include air-conditioning,

18 channel satellite TV, tea and coffee making facility, en-suite bathroom with shower and electronic laptop friendly safe.
